# **App Name**: Gold Coast Cleaners

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: Display a headline, subheadline, trust points, and CTAs for immediate engagement.
- Services Showcase: Highlight bond cleaning, carpet cleaning, and spring cleaning services with descriptions and CTAs.
- Quote Request Form: Enable users to request a quote by providing property details, contact information, and service preferences.
- Service Area Display: Showcase the suburbs served with a focus on local Gold Coast expertise.
- Bond Cleaning Details: Offer an SEO-optimized page detailing bond cleaning services, inclusions, and benefits.
- Contact Information: Provide clear contact information, including a phone number, email, and business hours.
- Sticky Header & Floating CTA: Implement a sticky header with the phone number and a floating 'Call Now' button for enhanced conversion.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy blue (#191970) for reliability and professionalism, referencing the 'James Bond' theme with a classic, trustworthy feel.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F0F0), a desaturated version of navy, offering a clean, modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#D4AF37) to evoke a premium 'James Bond' feel and draw attention to important elements.
- Body text and headlines: 'Inter', sans-serif, clean and modern font to communicate trust, experience and clarity. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use minimalist icons to represent cleaning services and benefits, maintaining a clutter-free design.
- Maintain a clean, white background with well-structured sections to ensure easy navigation and readability.
- Implement subtle animations for scrolling and transitions to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
